Roasted Cauliflower


    HOW TO ROAST WHOLE CAULIFLOWER
   
    Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit.
    Remove stem and any leaves from cauliflower head.
    Cover cauliflower in olive oil, rub in well.
    Season with salt and pepper.
    Place in baking dish and cover with foil.
    Roast for 30 minutes with foil.
    Remove foil and continue to roast until golden brown and softened, about 30 minutes.
    Remove from oven and let cool - cauliflower will be extremely hot.
    Slice and serve with BASIL PECAN PESTO and GOLDEN RAISINS.


    BASIL PECAN PESTO
    Yield: 1 qt 
    
    
    Pecans, toasted, cooled to room temp	175 grams
    Garlic, peeled  				        75 grams
    Salt						            15 grams
    Olive Oil					            2 cups
    Basil			 			            125 grams
    
    
    1.	Place pecans, garlic, salt, and blended oil in a food processor. Process until combined but still chunky.
    2.	Add basil and pulse until basil is combined into the mixture.
    3.	Do not over process, the pesto should still have some texture rather than being perfectly smooth.
    4.	Taste for seasoning, adjust if necessary.
    

    GOLDEN RAISINS
    Yield: 1 qt 


    Golden Raisins		1200 g
    White Balsamic		1000 g
    Sugar				225 g
    Salt				15 g


    1.	Combine all ingredients in sauce pot.
    2.	Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
    3.	Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.
