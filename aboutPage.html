I was born in Galveston, TX and grew up in Arlington, TX where I graduated high school. I went to Texas A&M and graduated with a degree in Information and Operations Management. I was hired directly out of college as the "IT guy" for a cancer research center. The job mostly required me to make sure the cancer center's Macintosh based network played nice with the hospital's Windows based network.

I started a job as a cook in College Station after doing some soul searching about what profession I would be happy working in. 2 years later I moved to Austin for culinary school and have been here ever since. I have had a fortunate career in restaurants, having worked at several restaurants considered to be among the best in Austin.

I have worked in restaurants for the past 20 years and am currently working towards another career change which is why I am enrolling in the Austin Coding Academy.
