I was born in Galveston, TX and grew up in Arlington, TX where I graduated high school. I went to Texas A&M and graduated with a degree in Information and Operations Management. I was hired directly out of college as the "IT guy" for a cancer research center. The job mostly required me to make sure the cancer center's Macintosh based network played nice with the hospital's Windows based network.

I decided to follow a passion of mine and started a job as a cook in College Station. 2 years later I moved to Austin for culinary school and have been here ever since. I have had a fortunate career in the Austin restaurant scene, having worked at several restaurants considered to be among the best in Austin.

I have worked as a chef for the past 20 years and currently working towards another career change. I took the leap a couple of months ago and am now studying software development at the Austin Coding Academy.
