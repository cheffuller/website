Brown Butter Ice Cream


    BROWN BUTTER ICE CREAM

    Heavy Cream             1 1/2 cups
    Half & Half             1 1/2 cups
    Dark Brown Sugar        1/3 cup
    Brown Butter Solids     2 tbsp
    Brandy                  2 tbsp
    Salt                    1/4 tsp
    
    Dextrose Powder         1/4 cup
    Sorbet Stabilizer       2 tsp
    
    Egg Yolks               6 each
    Dark Brown Sugar        1/3 cup
    
    1. Combine first 6 ingredients in sauce pot, turn on medium high heat.
    2. Mix dextrose and stabilizer.
    3. Whisk together egg yolks and brown sugar until thoroughly combined.
    4. Drizzle dextrose mixture into cream mixture while whisking.
    5. Temper cream mixture into yolk mixture.
    6. Return to sauce pot and heat, while whisking, to creme anglaise consistency.
    7. Refrigerate overnight.
    8. Spin in ice cream maker with ice cream paddle.
    9. Freeze for 2 hours, or overnight.
