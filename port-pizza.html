Ricotta Pizza


    PIZZA DOUGH
    Yield: 6 each 16 inch pizzas 
    
    Water, room temp		    986 grams
    Honey				        20 grams
    Active Dry Yeast		    9 grams
    
    AP Flour (Sir Galahad)	    1520 grams
    Red Wheat Flour		        80 grams
    Salt				        32 grams
    
    1.	Combine water and honey in stand mixing bowl and whisk together then add yeast.
    2.	Combine flours and salt and add to water mixture.
    3.	Mix on med speed for 10-15 minutes.
    4.	When dough passes the window test, cover and allow to rest for 10 minutes.
    5.	Portion dough into 520 gram balls.
    6.  Lightly oil dough balls and cover with plastic wrap.
    7.  Refrigerate overnight or up to three days.
    

    HOMEMADE RICOTTA
    Yield: 1.5 qt 
    
    Milk, whole		1 gallon
    Buttermilk		1.5 quarts
    Heavy Cream		1 cup
    
    Salt			As Needed
    
    1.  Set up a water bath with an immersion circulator to 195 deg F.
    2.	Combine milk, buttermilk, and cream in a pitcher and stir well to combine.
    3.	Divide the milk mixture evenly between cryovac bags and seal.
    4.	Cook bags in water bath for 1.75 hours.
    5.	Set up a colander lined with cheesecloth.
    6.	Carefully pour the contents of each bag into the cheesecloth.
    7.	Sprinkle salt over the top of the ricotta.
    8.	Allow ricotta to drain for approximately 20 minutes, depending on desired consistency.
    9.	Transfer ricotta to container and refrigerate for a couple of hours.
    

    WHITE PIZZA SAUCE
    Yield: 1.5 quarts 

    Butter                  45 grams
    AP Flour                45 grams

    Whole Milk			    1 quart

    Roasted Garlic		    100 grams
    Black Pepper, ground	2 grams

    Parmesan, grated		250 grams

    Salt				    To Taste

    1.	In a sauce pot over medium high heat, melt butter and add in the flour.
    2.  Cook, stirring frequently, until roux is light brown.
    3.	Add milk in 3 batches, whisking well with each addition.
    4.  Add in roasted garlic and black pepper and bring to a simmer.
    5.	Add in parmesan while whisking.
    6.	Remove from heat and blend, either with an immersion blender or in the vitamix.
    7.	Taste and adjust with salt if needed.
    8.	Transfer to a container and refrigerate overnight.
