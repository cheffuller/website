Ricotta Pizza


    PIZZA DOUGH
    Yield: 6 each 16 inch pizzas 
    
    Water, room temp		    986 grams
    Honey				        20 grams
    Active Dry Yeast		    9 grams
    
    AP Flour (Sir Galahad)	    1520 grams
    Red Wheat Flour		        80 grams
    Salt				        32 grams
    
    
    1.	Combine water and honey in stand mixing bowl and whisk together then add yeast.
    2.	Combine flours and salt and add to water mixture.
    3.	Mix on med speed for 10-15 minutes.
    4.	When dough passes the window test, cover and allow to rest for 10 minutes.
    5.	Portion dough into 520 gram balls.
    6.  Lightly oil dough balls and cover with plastic wrap.
    7.  Refrigerate overnight or up to three days.
    

    Homemade Ricotta
    Yield: 1.5 qt 
    
                
    Milk, whole		1 gallon
    Buttermilk		1.5 quarts
    Heavy Cream		1 cup
    
    Salt			As Needed
    
    1.  Set up a water bath with an immersion circulator to 195 deg F.
    2.	Combine milk, buttermilk, and cream in a pitcher and stir well to combine.
    3.	Divide the milk mixture between cryovac bags .
    4.	Cook for 1.75 hours.
    5.	Set up the deep hotel pan with a perforated drain pan lined with cheesecloth.
    6.	Carefully pour the contents of each bag into the cheesecloth.
    7.	Sprinkle salt over the top of the ricotta.
    8.	Transfer drain pan to a 4‚Äù hotel pan.
    9.	Allow ricotta to drain in refrigerator for approximately 1 hour.
    10.	Transfer ricotta to quarts, label, date, and refrigerate.
